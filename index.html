JOSEPH NIELSEN

Joseph Nielsen is 35 years old and was born and raised in Orange County, CA. After graduating from Le Cordon Bleu, London in 2006 he began working as a private chef and spent the following 8 years working his way through the food & beverage industry in San Francisco.

Upon returning to Southern California in late 2016 he quickly jumped into the emerging food scene working again as a private chef, and running an Italian restaurant in Costa Mesa.

After completing the UCI Coding Bootcamp Joseph plans to utilize his newly honed skills in conjunction with his background in the food & beverage industry to develop new applications and technologies in the underserved market.
